Innovation in technology continues to accelerate at a rapid pace.  Advances in personal computers, smartphones, the internet, social networking, 3-D printing, virtual and augmented reality, and artificial intelligence, just to name a few, have already made a significant impact on many aspects of our daily lives. These technologies have impacted the field of education as well. Of course, the availability of these technologies will vary greatly between different schools and cultures.

One clear example of how technology can positively impact learning is the ability to connect and collaborate with experts and other students across the globe.

This course is a great example.  Bringing together aspiring and experienced educators to share ideas and gain insight into how to become an effective teacher requires only an internet connection and a web browser.
