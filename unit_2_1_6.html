Your First Conference

Throughout this training course you will be invited to multiple group conferences as well as individual one-on-one meetings with the facilitators.

The conferences are announced in advanced to give you time to plan and attend. In order to have a successful experience with conferences, make sure you are:

  • sitting in a quiet and appropriate environment to attend the conference
  • using a headset to minimize background echo
  • keeping your microphone on mute and turning it on only when planning to speak

All conferences will be recorded and if you are not able to attend the group conferences live, you will have access to watch the recording. If the conference is planned as a one-on-one meeting and you are unable to attend at the scheduled time and date, please notify the facilitator to reschedule.
