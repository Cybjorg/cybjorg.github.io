Variety of Concerns

Parent concerns (complaints, criticisms etc.) can relate to your teaching methods, organization skills, student communications just to mention a few. The complaints could be based on their personal frustrations, such as trying to figure out what the homework really is for their child, or falsified information brought to the parent in defense of the student's own mistakes.

In addition to the underlying cause of the complaint, the personality of the parent will impact the nature of the criticism. In this blog post by experienced teacher Max Fischer, he describes parents as "The Good, The Bad and The Ugly." The post is outdated and does not present an ideal way to deal with students, but the point about parents being different is valid.
