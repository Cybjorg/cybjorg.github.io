Journal Activity

Several times during this course you will be asked to add to your teaching journal. This journal will be one way to show your engagement and progress, and will also be an effective way for you to develop your reflection skills. Remember, reflection is one of the key teacher competencies for success.

Each time you are prompted within the course to complete a journal entry, open this document and insert your reflection into the relevant section. This journal will be shared only with the course facilitator. Make sure you keep this journal in a safe place so that you can add to it as required.
