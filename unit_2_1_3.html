Activity

In this activity, you will be creating a Word Cloud, similar to the example in the image on the right.

Instructions
  1. List the top 10 teacher characteristics that you think a good teacher must have.
  2. Go to Word It Out.com and type the 10 characteristics in the text box. Select "Generate" to create your Word Cloud.
  3. Design your Word Cloud. Change the font styles, color, layout. Once finished, save your work.
  4. You will be prompted to enter a valid email address then validate your account.
  5. A link to your Word Cloud will be sent to your email address. Copy the link and share it with your classmates in this wiki. 
  6. Once ready to contribute to the wiki, select the "edit" button located at the top right corner, add your name and the link to your Word Cloud. Don't forget save your work!
