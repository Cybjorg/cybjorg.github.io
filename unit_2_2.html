What Makes an Effective Teacher?

An effective teacher educates students, inspires them, learns from them, and makes a change in their lives.

Take a moment to think of one teacher that made an impact on your learning experience and life. Why did that specific teacher make an impact on you?

What is Expected from Teachers?

  • Explain material and help students learn new concept and skills
  • Understand student differences: learning styles, culture, background, and interests
  • Manage classroom: be authoritative and respectful
  • Communicate with administration and parents
  • Provide constructive feedback 
  • Continuous learning and enhancements
